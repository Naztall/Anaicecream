Our Story

ANA was born from a passion to create ice cream experiences unlike any other. Our founder, Ana herself, traveled the world collecting unique flavor inspirations from every corner of the globe.

Each of our exotic flavors is crafted with premium ingredients and traditional techniques from their country of origin, combined with modern ice cream artistry.

We believe ice cream should be an adventure, a moment of pure joy that transports you to new places with every spoonful.
